About CropCare AI

Revolutionizing Cocoa Disease Management in Cameroon

Our Mission

To empower Cameroon cocoa farmers with advanced AI technology for early disease detection and effective crop management, ultimately increasing yields and improving livelihoods.

CropCare AI is designed specifically for the challenges faced by cocoa farmers in the South and South-West regions of Cameroon, where black pod disease and other cocoa diseases cause significant yield losses.

The Problem We're Solving

Black Pod Disease Impact

Cameroon's cocoa industry faces a crisis with black pod disease causing 20-30% annual losses, potentially reaching 100% in severe cases. Heavy rains since July 2025 have triggered infection rates of 65-70% in key districts.

Production Decline

National cocoa production is expected to contract by at least 10% in the rainy season. The South-West region, supplying nearly half the crop, is hit hardest with pod losses ranging from 30% to 90%.

Our Solution

AI-powered disease detection system using advanced machine learning algorithms and IoT technology.

  • Early detection with 95% accuracy
  • Real-time analysis through smartphone cameras
  • IoT sensors for environmental monitoring
  • Personalized treatment recommendations
  • Predictive analytics for outbreak forecasting
